Through Obscurity

When you see a door that has a number pad or even a retina scanner next to it you know that there is something important hiding behind that door. In AR-Access all of the authentication is performed in AR meaning the door itself doesnâ€™t have to reveal the fact that there is something important behind it. This is something that we refer to as Security Through Obscurity.

Code
Scrambling

In order to make it impossible for bystanders to figure out your passcode we add a factor of randomness to the authentication process. The position of the numbers is scrambled between interactions, this makes it impossible for anyone not within Artificial Reality to figure out your passcode.
