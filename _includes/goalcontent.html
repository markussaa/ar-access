What did we try to achieve with this project?

Design For The Real World

Physical Access

Design for
the real world

The intention of the project was to create a product that is comparable with various solutions that are available today and that address all the issues of desirability, feasibility and viability. Considering that the prototype we ended up with in the end is not far away from a functioning implementation, and based on our evaluation covers all the aforementioned criteria we think we have cover this goal successfully.

Design with
innovation in mind

One of our goals was to create a solution that feels innovative and utilizes new technology. Our research didn’t reveal any similar solutions on the market and there didn’t seem to be much research done within AR authorization, there we deemed this goal as successful.

Improved
Security

AR-Access improves on security by providing “security through obscurity” and “scrambling”, which are not commonly seen in the typical solutions found today. Although the features have not been evaluated on to what degree they contribute to the security aspect, we still consider this goal as fulfilled based on the feedback we got during our prototype evaluations.
